3Pl impersonal forms may refer to constructions containing a non-referential 3Pl pronominal subject (e.g. English *they*) 
or only verbal inflection ([](Source?ref&with_internal_ref_link#cldf:siewierska_2010)). This question is concerned with the latter, i.e. the expression of impersonal 
reference by means of 3Pl verb forms, as in examples (1) and (2). 

[](ExampleTable?example_id=1&with_internal_ref_link#cldf:UT039-1)

[](ExampleTable?example_id=2&with_internal_ref_link#cldf:UT039-2)

## Coding

The value is '1' if 3Pl verb forms can be used for impersonal reference.
