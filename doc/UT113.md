**Are there different words for younger and older relatives for relations other than siblings?**

This question asks whether kinship terms distinguish older and younger relatives, apart from siblings, as in North Saami.

North Saami<br/>
>*eahki* 'father's older brother'<br/>
>*čeahci* 'father's younger brother'<br/>
>*goaski* 'mother's older sister'<br/>
>*muoŧŧá* 'mother's younger sister'<br/>

**Coding.** The answer is '1' if there are separate words and they are in use in the language.
