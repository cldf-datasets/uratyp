**Is there an animacy distinction for both concepts: 'new/young' and 'old'?**

This question asks whether the choice of words meaning 'old' and 'new/young' depends on animacy, as in Hungarian.

Hungarian<br/>

>*régi film : öreg ember*<br/>
>'old movie' : 'old man'<br/>

>*új ruha : fiatal férfi*<br/>
>'new dress' vs. 'young man'<br/>

**Coding.** The answer is '1' if there are separate words for both concepts. The answer is '0' if there is no distinction based on animacy, or if a distinction is made for only one of the concepts.
