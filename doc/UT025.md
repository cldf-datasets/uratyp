**Is there morphological marking on the verb dedicated to the expression of a desire or wish?**

Desiderative modality expresses a desire or wish of the subject referent to bring about a particular state of affairs. For example, Mari has a separate desiderative mood marked with *-ne* (1); it has no verb meaning 'to want' (Riese et al. 2019:238-246). (The term *optative* may also be used.)

(1) Meadow Mari<br/>
>*луднем : луднет* etc.<br/>
>lud-ne-m : lud-ne-t etc.<br/>
>read-DES-1SG : read-DES-2SG<br/>
>‘I, you etc. want to/intend to read'<br/>
(Riese et al. 2019:238)

**Coding.** The value is '1' if there is a dedicated marker for the expression of a desire or wish and the marker is available with at least some lexical verbs and it occurs in different persons. Examples of a conditional mood used in a similar meaning do not suffice for '1', since the situation is regarded as possible only under certain conditions.
